About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University with a Doctorate in Engineering. My research focuses on modeling and monitoring agricultural ecosystems, particularly under soil salinity stress.
I use remote sensing, irrigation modeling, and data-driven approaches to study plant fertilization and water management. I'm especially interested in high-resolution evapotranspiration mapping and sustainable crop production in heterogeneous croplands.
